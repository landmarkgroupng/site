Long-Range Gold Detector with Android Smartphone

The Long Range is used to locate buried objects at long distances. The exact position of the object located can be determined using a cross-section. The Bionic X4 Gold Long Range Detector has been developed especially for gold explorers, gold prospectors, and treasure hunters looking for gold at distances. In contrast to traditional long-range detectors, the Bionic X4 can be combined with Android Smart Phone to visually locate gold targets.

The fully automated Bluetooth connection between the Smart Phone and the Bionic X4 transmits scan data directly to the Smart Phone, where the data is mapped to the display. So through the Smart Phone camera, the treasure hunter can see the bearing, direction, and picture and fix it for subsequent analysis.

The Bionic X4 Gold Long Range Detector achieves the best performance in sparsely populated areas. Since the Bionic X4 is very sensitive to changes in ion and bionic fields, accuracy increases significantly in undisturbed or undeveloped areas.

Features and characteristics

Classic: Long-range gold detector with two different detection methods and visual location software to locate hidden gold treasures at long distances.
